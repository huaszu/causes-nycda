Ketchup

Recipe for Ketchup

  • 2 standard-size packets of ketchup
  • 10 waffle fries

Squeeze ketchup onto fries. Enjoy. Be sure to maintain the optimal ratio of one packet of ketchup per five waffle fries.

Recipe for Mustard

  • 3 standard-size packets of mustard
  • 2 frankfurters
  • 0 buns

Carefully put 1.5 packets' worth of mustard on each frankfurter. This recipe is a buns-free recipe, in honor of our gluten-free friends. We recommend splitting this food among two people.

Recipe for Barbecue

  • 5 standard-size packets of barbecue sauce
  • 5 barbecue chicken wings
  • 1 gallon of water in a ceramic pot
  1. Dip 1 chicken wing in the water. Rinse the sauce off the wing.
  2. Dip the wing in the barbecue sauce. True wing-eaters put on their own sauce.
  3. Repeat until you have consumed all wings and all sauce.

Recipe for Ranch

  • 10 standard-size packets of ranch
  • 1 heartful of ceaseless love for ranch
  1. Open one packet of ranch and consume in one gulp.
  2. Repeat until you have consumed everything. Except for the plastic packaging.
